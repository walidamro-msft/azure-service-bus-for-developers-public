Tier overview

Service Bus offers three primary tiers: Basic, Standard, and Premium. The right tier depends on throughput, latency consistency, advanced features, and compliance/network constraints.

TierBest forKey limits
BasicSimple queue workloadsNo topics/subscriptions or advanced features
StandardGeneral enterprise messagingShared compute, variable latency under contention
PremiumMission-critical and isolated workloadsHigher baseline cost, dedicated messaging units

Cost and governance practice

  • Start with Standard for most dev/test environments.
  • Promote to Premium for strict latency SLOs and noisy-neighbor avoidance.
  • Tag namespaces with owner, environment, and cost center for chargeback.
  • Use alerts on incoming requests, throttles, and DLQ growth.
